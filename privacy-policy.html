PRIVACY POLICY

Last Updated: February 6, 2026

TL;DR: Scroll Pause doesn't collect, store, or transmit any of your data. Everything stays on your device. We don't track you. We don't sell your data. We don't even know you're using the app.

Information Collection and Use

Scroll Pause does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. All data related to your screen time usage, budgets, and settings is stored locally on your device using your device's storage.

Data Storage

The following data is stored locally on your device:

  • Your daily screen time budget preference
  • Time used today
  • Lock/unlock status
  • Session timestamps

This data never leaves your device. It is not backed up to our servers because we don't have servers. If you delete the app, all data is permanently deleted.

Third-Party Services

Scroll Pause does not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising services. We don't integrate with Facebook, Google Analytics, or any other tracking platforms.

Notifications

The app requests permission to send local notifications to alert you when your screen time budget is used up. These notifications are generated locally on your device and do not involve any external servers.

Permissions

Scroll Pause requests the following permissions:

  • Notifications: To alert you when time is up
  • Background processing: To keep the timer running when the app is closed

No other permissions are requested or required.

Children's Privacy

Scroll Pause does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13. Since we don't collect any data at all, there is no risk of collecting children's data.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Data Deletion

To delete all your data, simply delete the app from your device. Since all data is stored locally, deleting the app permanently removes all associated data.
